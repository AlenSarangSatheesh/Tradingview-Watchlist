## Detailed description

Get unlimited watchlists for TradingView — free.

TradingView's free plan limits how many watchlists you can create. This extension keeps its own watchlists in Chrome's side panel, so you can create as many watchlists as you want and still open every chart on TradingView with one click. Works with any TradingView plan, including the free one.

FEATURES

• Unlimited watchlists — create, rename, reorder and delete as many watchlists as you need
• One-click charts — click any symbol to open or switch its TradingView chart; press Space to cycle through the list
• Chartink screener import — pull every stock from a Chartink screener into a watchlist named after that screener, with one button on the screener page
• One-click re-sync — imported screeners remember their Chartink URL; refresh the list with today's results anytime
• CSV import & export — bulk-add symbols from a CSV file, or export a watchlist to move it to another machine
• Search, sort & drag — filter symbols as you type, sort A–Z, reorder with drag-and-drop
• Add from TradingView — a floating "Add to Watchlist" button on TradingView pages saves the current symbol to any list
• Dark & light themes — match your TradingView setup

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Your watchlists never leave your device. No account, no analytics, no tracking, no ads. The only network requests are to chartink.com (when you import or re-sync a screener) and tradingview.com (when you open a chart).

HOW IT WORKS

1. Install the extension and open it from Chrome's side panel
2. Create a watchlist with the + button — one per strategy, sector, or timeframe
3. Add symbols from a Chartink screener, a CSV file, or the floating Add button on any TradingView page
4. Click a symbol to open its TradingView chart; press Space to jump to the next one
