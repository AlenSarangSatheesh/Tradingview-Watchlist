Unlimited Watchlists for TradingView

A lightweight Chrome side-panel that lets you organize an unlimited number of TradingView-style watchlists, import symbols from CSV or a Chartink screener, and open any chart on TradingView with a single click.

Add to Chrome Coming soon to the Chrome Web Store.

Features

📋 Unlimited watchlists

Create, rename, reorder and delete as many watchlists as you need — right in the browser side panel.

📈 One-click charts

Click any symbol to instantly open or switch its chart on TradingView. Press Space to cycle through a list.

🔎 Import from Chartink

Pull every stock from a Chartink screener into a watchlist named after that screener — one button on the screener page or from the side panel.

🗂️ CSV import

Bulk-add symbols from a CSV file. Imports create or update a watchlist named after the file.

⚡ Search, sort & drag

Filter symbols as you type, sort A–Z, and reorder watchlists with drag-and-drop.

🌗 Dark & light themes

Switch between dark and light mode from Settings. Your choice is remembered.

🔒 Private by design

Your watchlists stay on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

➕ Add from TradingView

An "Add to Watchlist" button on TradingView pages lets you save the current symbol to any list.

How it works

  1. Open the extension from the Chrome side panel.
  2. Click New to create a watchlist.
  3. Add stocks via Import (CSV or Chartink), or the floating Add to Watchlist button on any TradingView page.
  4. Click a symbol to open its chart on TradingView. Press Space to move to the next one.

Privacy

This extension stores your watchlists locally on your device and does not collect personal information, use analytics, or sell data. The only network requests it makes are to chartink.com (when you import a screener) and to tradingview.com (to open charts).
